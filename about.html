Why?

Vanta exists to make your development process smoother.

LuaU is already powerful, but it can sometimes be verbose and unforgiving with variables.

Developers often find themselves struggling with errors that arise simply from managing data incorrectly.

Vanta eliminates much of this struggle by offering a structured, simplified layer on top of LuaU.

With Vanta, you spend less time debugging and more time building.

It introduces cleaner syntax for variable storage, making your scripts easier to read and maintain.

Each line of your code becomes more predictable, reducing surprises in production.

The design of Vanta emphasizes clarity, meaning new developers can learn quickly and professionals can move faster.

It is not a replacement for LuaU, but rather a guiding framework that helps you avoid its common pitfalls.

In short: Vanta exists because we believe coding in Roblox should be intuitive, stable, and fun.
